Crash Bandicoot's moves

Crash Bandicoot is a cartoony platformer released in the year 1996 by Naughty Dog and stars a marsupial named Crash Bandicoot, who was an animal evolved into a super-sapient being using the Evolvo-Ray. With this evolution Crash Bandicoot gained a few abilities throughout his adventures and we're going to go over them from the first 3 games and explain how they are used and what they do.

The purpose of this simple website is to showcase how Crash's platforming abilities have changed and evolved over the first 3 games and what new abilities he's picked up from defeating various bosses (in the third game specifically) as well as how this has allowed the games to be more diverse and adventurous allowing Naughty Dog to developed more complicated and difficult levels to fit the new abilities.

Crash Bandicoot 1 moves

Bandicoot's can jump!

There's one thing this Bandicoot can do well, and that's jump! With a simple press of the "X" button on your controller (provided you're playing this on a Sony Playstation console) allows Crash to jump for a set amount of time over a set amount of distance. This allows you to cross gaps, jump over (or on!) enemies and even jump on crates to release the goodies within.
